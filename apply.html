Apply for Job

There are multiple approaches to discovering job opportunities. Here are six proven methods to find QA roles and get your application noticed.

1. Facebook Groups 💻

Joining relevant groups on Facebook can help you discover job openings and connect with potential employers.

IT JOBS VACANCY (NEPAL) →

2. LinkedIn 🌐

LinkedIn is the #1 professional networking site. Search for positions, connect with hiring managers, and join industry groups. Keep your profile updated and turn on "Open to Work."

Search Jobs on LinkedIn →

3. Job Portals 🔍

Nepal-based job boards where many tech companies post openings regularly.

MeroJob → Jobaxle → Jobjee →

4. Career Pages 🏢

Apply directly on company career pages. Many offer internship programmes with potential for full-time conversion.

  • Leapfrog Technology — Internship programme with potential full-time conversion
  • Ekbana — Similar internship structure
  • CedarGate
  • Cotiviti Nepal
  • Deerhold
  • 8 Square I
  • Bajra Technologies
  • SwagApp

5. Tech Events 📅

Attending industry events, conferences, and networking events can help you learn about job openings and make connections with potential employers. Many companies hire directly from events — bring your CV and business cards.

6. Job Fairs 🎪

Attending job fairs is a great way to meet potential employers and learn about openings. You get to make a face-to-face impression, which is rare in today's digital-first hiring landscape.

💡 Pro Tip: Timing Your Application

Submit your applications on Monday mornings before office hours. Your application appears first in the reviewer's queue and benefits from the post-weekend fresh mindset of hiring managers. This small change can significantly increase your response rate.
