Apply for a Job

There are multiple paths to discovering job opportunities. Here are six proven methods to find QA roles and get your application noticed.

1. Facebook Groups 💻

Joining relevant Facebook groups can help you discover job openings and connect directly with hiring managers in your region.

IT Jobs Vacancy (Nepal) →

2. LinkedIn 🌐

LinkedIn is the #1 professional networking platform. Search for QA positions, connect with hiring managers, and join industry groups. Keep your profile updated and turn on "Open to Work" — recruiters actively search for candidates.

Search Jobs on LinkedIn →

3. Job Portals 🔍

Nepal-based job boards where many tech companies post openings regularly.

MeroJob → Jobaxle → Jobejee →

4. Company Career Pages 🏢

Apply directly through company websites. Many offer structured internship programmes with potential for full-time conversion — and competition is typically lower than on job boards.

  • Leapfrog Technology — Internship programme with potential full-time conversion
  • Ekbana — Similar internship structure
  • CedarGate
  • Cotiviti Nepal
  • Deerhold
  • 8 Square I
  • Bajra Technologies
  • SwagApp

5. Tech Events & Meetups 📅

Attending industry conferences, meetups, and networking events exposes you to companies that hire directly at these events. Bring copies of your CV and a few business cards — a face-to-face impression is rare and valuable in today's digital-first world.

6. Job Fairs 🎪

Job fairs let you meet multiple employers in a single day. You get to make direct impressions, ask real questions, and often receive instant feedback on your CV — an opportunity that online applications simply can't replicate.

💡 Pro Tip: Timing Your Application

Submit applications on Monday mornings before office hours. Your application appears near the top of the recruiter's queue and benefits from their fresh, post-weekend mindset. This one small habit can noticeably increase your response rate.
